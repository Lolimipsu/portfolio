Takagi-san Fan Visual Novel Game

A fan visual novel game based on Soichirou Yamamoto's work.

❮
❯

Project Overview

In this project, I led and coordinated a talented international team of 26 individuals, including developers and digital artists, in the creation of a fan game based on the "Skilled Teaser Takagi-san" series. As both project lead and programming lead, I supervised the development process, ensuring the production of clean, readable code that met high standards of quality and efficiency. My role also involved overseeing the artistic contributions, ensuring the visual elements complemented the game's narrative seamlessly.

Utilizing Python and Ren'Py as the primary programming languages, I guided the team through various development tasks, achieving a polished and engaging visual novel experience. The project was a collaborative effort that showcased our collective creativity and technical skills, resulting in a product that faithfully captured the spirit of the original series while offering an interactive and immersive gameplay experience for the fans of the series.
