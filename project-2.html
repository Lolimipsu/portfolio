Project Overview

In this project, I Developed a Discord bot with AI integration to enhance user engagement and interaction. The bot offers a range of features, including personalized conversations tailored to users' preferences and interests.

It also performs image manipulation, creating personalized "thank you" notifications for users who boost the server. Additionally, the bot can respond to requests for endearments, such as sending a goodnight kiss image and mentioning the user.

For those who enjoy roleplaying, the bot can engage in various scenarios, adding a fun and interactive element to the user experience. This project showcases the integration of AI to create a dynamic and engaging environment for Discord users.
